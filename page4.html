About The Founder 

Developing Mindz was founded on the core principals of helping students reach their full potential. The key is understanding that, not all students are the same. This means that their way of acquiring a knowledge base or fundamental skills differ.

Being a qualified Educator in the education field for over 15 years, with 2 teaching licenses (South African and Canadian), I realized that there is an evident gap, which unfortunately affects students who learn and acquire knowledge differently. Being a teacher who has taught in 3 countries at Primary, Intermediate and High school levels and being in an HOD position, managing over 500 students at primary level , I have recognized that our biggest weakness is the inability to provide the adequate tools needed for students of all learning styles.

I have always had a passion for education as I truly believe that Education is liberating and creates opportunities beyond belief.

It is for this reason that I have followed my yearning for knowledge and opened my horizons to a huge amount of experience both locally and internationally. This has fuelled my ‘educational hunger’ to gain experience in 5 different curriculums (South African CAPS curriculum, American Curriculum, Canadian Curriculum, British Cambridge Curriculum and The French Curriculum.)

This has further given me the realization that despite teaching in 3 countries, teaching 5 different curriculums and despite the social, economic, and personal issues that differ, one thing remained the same. With the pace of school, the time constraints, lack of resources, class numbers, admin work and deadlines, catering to all student needs especially those who have gaps in their knowledge base can at times be a very difficult task to accomplish.

It is for this reason that I have decided to create Developing Mindz Education centre. This was in the hope of not only offering tuition to supplement student progress but highlight a specialized learning program which incorporates a vast range of differentiated systems and methodologies aimed towards helping students reach the recommended grade level expectation.

From the wealth of knowledge which I have gained, the various professional development courses that I have completed, paired together with the exposure to different educational cultures and backgrounds, I have pieced together an effective program to assist with student needs.

It is my hope that through developing Mindz students are able to bridge the gap and, have an opportunity to educate themselves to their full potential.
